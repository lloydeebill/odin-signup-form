Fushimi Inari Taisha

Located in Kyoto, Japan, is the head shrine of the kami Inari, the Shinto god of rice, prosperity, and foxes. Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates forming scenic trails up Mount Inari, the shrine attracts millions of visitors annually who come to experience its spiritual significance and picturesque beauty.
